DNAS Connect App

DNAS Connect is an app Day & Nite / All Service built in house for our customers. 
We at Day & Nite appreciate your business and your time. We know waiting on a phone 
call can be frustrating and inefficient. That is the reason why we are introducing 
our App “DNAS Connect” to our users: “DNAS Connect” lets you place new service 
requests and track existing requests status easily. You can download the app today! 
Search “DNAS Connect” on your Apple App Store or Google Play Store. Alternatively, 
for iOS, the download link is https://apps.apple.com/us/app/dnas-lite/id1531944898 
and for Android, the download link is https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.dayniteit.dnaslite&pcampaignid=web_share. 
For our non-contract customers, please use the non-contract customer service request
webform to submit a new work request: https://www.wearetheone.com/servicerequest/.
